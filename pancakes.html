Pancakes

Description

This recipe doesn't require much thought early in the morning, and tastes great!

And, of course, it's nothing more than some simple and classic Pancakes... What could go wrong right?

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 egg, beaten, just as your dad used to do with you
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder and salt. Make a well in the center, and pour in milk, egg and oil. Mix until smooth.
  2. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium high heat. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake. Brown on both sides and serve hot.
